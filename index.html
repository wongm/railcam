Location and passing rail traffic

This camera is located at the north end of Sunshine railway station, where the lines west towards Geelong and Ballarat branch off from those headed north to Bendigo and north-east towards Seymour

In the foreground are the Regional Rail Link tracks used by V/Line trains to Geelong, Ballarat and Bendigo, followed by electrified tracks for Metro Trains Melbourne services to Sunbury, and finally the North-East standard gauge that runs between Melbourne and Sydney.

Technology

This webcam uses a Dahua IP camera pointed at the railway line, and connected to a Raspberry Pi 3 running ffmpeg to stream the live footage to YouTube.

The code that runs this site can be found at https://github.com/wongm/railcam/.
